# Art Direction Plan

## Visual Direction

- readable pixel-art presentation
- simple but distinct zone identity
- lightweight HUD that does not obscure play
- visual feedback for progression and district quality

## Priorities

- strengthen map readability
- reinforce progression through badges, bars, and environmental cues
- keep low-resolution assets visually coherent when scaled

## Constraints

- only ship assets with clear redistribution rights
- keep runtime loading simple
- avoid heavy external art pipelines in the public portfolio snapshot
